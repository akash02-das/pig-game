Game Rules

Rule 1: The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.

Rule 2: In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score.

Rule 3: BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.

Rule 4: A player looses his ENTIRE score when he rolls two 6 in a row. After that, it's the next player's turn.

Rule 5: The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn.

Rule 6: players can set the winning score, so that they can change the predefined score of 100.

Rule 7: The first player to reach winning points on GLOBAL score wins the game.
